Our Services

Routine Exams

It is important that you visit your dentist on a regular basis to detect and treat any dental problems before you lose a tooth.

Dental Hygiene

Regular cleaning and prophylaxis done by our experienced hygienists will help you reduce common dental problems.

Dental Fillings

We use top of the line materials which blend in with your tooth colour so that you never have to let a cavity bother you again.

Dentures

Protect that smile with our fitted dentures and enhance the way your mouth feels and looks.

Porcelain Crowns

A dental crown is a tooth-shaped "cap" that is placed over the original tooth to restore its shape and size, strength, and improve its appearance.

Teeth Whitening

Try our take-home teeth whitening kit and get back your self confidence and that bright beautiful smile!
